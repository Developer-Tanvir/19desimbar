Love History Chip? Support us to keep bringing you stories from people from all world.

History Chip depends on everyone’s stories and support. The more stories the better the site. But the bigger the archive, the more resources are required to keep all the gears running smoothly. Your donations will help History Chip grow and thrive and fulfill all the long term goals we have for making this web site an amazing, ground breaking project. Your donations will help History Chip raise the voices of all people all over the world, including those whose voices couldn’t be heard before, while, at the same time, it fulfills its promise to provide more truth to history.

Your donations will give all these stories lasting impact! Here’s how:

We are hard at work developing audio, video, and an app, all to make story-telling easier and more accessible for people around the world. Additionally, we are working on lots of new projects related to story-telling, writing, community and education. Your donations will help us increase our server size, maintain our archive, enhance our programs, and continually improve our functionality.

Are there other ways to help?

Adding your stories to History Chip is a wonderful and important way to help. You can also interview your elderly friends and family, or help them share their stories on History Chip. Their stories are so precious and will disappear if they aren’t recorded. Adding them to History Chip will not only include them in history but will preserve those important pieces of stories. All of these stories are vital additions to the truth of the world’s history. You can also help to get the word out about History Chip by telling your friends and family about it and spread the word on your social media.

Thank you for supporting this important project!

Jean McGavin Founder, History Chip
